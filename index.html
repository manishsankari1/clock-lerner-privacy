Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

Welcome to Clock Learner for Kids. This educational application is designed to help children aged 4 years and above learn how to read analog clocks in a fun, simple, and engaging way.

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with Google Play's Families Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or request any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Photos or videos
  • Contacts
  • Location information
  • Payment information

2. Children's Privacy

Clock Learner for Kids is intended for children aged 4 years and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app follows Google's Families Policy requirements. Parents or guardians are encouraged to supervise children's use of mobile applications.

3. Internet Access

The app requires an internet connection to display advertisements provided by Google AdMob and to access Google Play services. The app itself does not collect or store personal information.

4. Advertising

This application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may automatically collect limited information such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID (where permitted)
  • IP address
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • App performance and diagnostic information

This information is collected directly by Google to provide, improve, and measure advertisements. The developer of Clock Learner for Kids does not collect, store, or access this information.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Analytics

This application does not use Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, or any other third-party analytics service.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. Any information automatically collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy.

7. Security

Because the application does not collect personal information directly, no personal user information is stored by the developer.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be published on this page along with the updated revision date.
